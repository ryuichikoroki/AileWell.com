Privacy Policy

AileWell Inc. (“we,” “our,” or “the Company”) recognizes the protection of personal information as an important responsibility and handles it appropriately in accordance with this policy.

1. Definition of personal information

“Personal information” in this policy means personal information as defined in the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (Japan). It includes information that can identify a specific individual, such as name, address, telephone number, and email address.

2. Collection of personal information

We may collect personal information in the following cases:

  • When you use our contact form
  • When you apply for services or request a quote
  • When you apply for employment
  • Other cases related to transactions or business with us

3. Purpose of use

We use collected personal information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and consultations
  • Providing services and performing contracts
  • Communications related to recruitment
  • Service improvement and information about new services
  • Fulfillment of legal obligations

4. Provision to third parties

We do not provide your personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • When you have given consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary for the protection of life, body, or property

5. Security measures

We take appropriate security measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information. We also supervise employees who handle personal information appropriately.

6. Disclosure, correction, and deletion

You may request disclosure, correction, deletion, or suspension of use of your personal information held by us. Please contact us through the contact form for such requests.

7. Use of cookies

Our website may use cookies for purposes such as access analysis. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may become unavailable.

8. Changes to this policy

We may change this policy as needed. The revised policy will take effect when posted on this website.
