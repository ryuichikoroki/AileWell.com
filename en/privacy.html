Privacy Policy

AileWell Co., Ltd. (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you share with us.

1. What We Mean by Personal Information

Personal information refers to any data that can identify a specific individual, such as your name, address, phone number, or email address, as defined under Japan’s Act on the Protection of Personal Information.

2. When We Collect Your Information

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Submit an inquiry through our contact form
  • Request a service or quote
  • Apply for a position with us
  • Engage in any other business transaction with us

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Delivering our services and fulfilling contractual obligations
  • Processing job applications and recruitment communications
  • Improving our services and sharing relevant updates
  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law or regulation
  • When necessary to protect life, safety, or property

5. How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or damage to your personal information. All employees who handle personal data are trained and supervised accordingly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or suspension of use of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact form.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics and to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as expected.

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will take effect once posted on this page.
